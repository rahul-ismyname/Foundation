Admission Process

Simple 3-step process to get started.

Step 1: Registration

Visit our office and fill out the registration form. You can also download the form from our website and submit it at the office.

Step 2: Assessment (Optional)

For certain batches, we conduct a basic scholarship test to understand the student's current level. This helps us provide personalized attention.

Step 3: Enrollment

Complete the fee payment and documentation process to confirm the seat. Study materials will be provided on the same day.

Documents Required

  • 2 Passport size photographs
  • Copy of Aadhar Card
  • Previous year's mark sheet
